# Troubleshooting Balances, Transfers, and Interest

If something doesn't look right with your Balances, Transfers, and Interest, check this guide first before reporting an issue to your teacher.

## If your balance looks wrong, check these first:
- Are you viewing the correct class? Balances are per join code.
- Did your teacher void a recent transaction? Voided items disappear from totals.
- Are you looking at the correct account (checking vs savings)? They are separate ledgers.
- If your savings balance is negative, a teacher must have posted a manual transaction. Transfers cannot overdraw savings.

## If a transfer fails, check these first:
- Is Banking enabled for your class period?
- Did you enter the correct passphrase?
- Do you have enough funds in the source account?
- Did you choose different accounts for the sender and receiver?

## This is normal and expected when:
- Savings interest posts monthly, not instantly.
- Interest does not post on days when you moved money into savings.
- Overdraft protection or fees allow a negative checking balance if enabled by your teacher.

## When to report an issue to your teacher
If you've checked everything above and the problem is still occurring:
- Tell them the class you were in when the transaction happened.
- Tell them the exact error message from the transfer form.
- Use the "Report Issue" button on the Help page or next to the transaction.
