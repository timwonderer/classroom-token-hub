# Troubleshooting Help and Issue Reports

If something doesn't look right with your Help and Issue Reports, check this guide first before speaking to your teacher.

## If you cannot submit an issue, check these first:
- Have you selected a class? Issues are tied to the current join code.
- Does the transaction or tap event belong to your current class?
- Are you trying to view another student's issue list? Issues are scoped per student.

## This is normal and expected when:
- New issues show up with a pending status until your teacher reviews them.
- Transaction issues are limited to your own transactions.

## When to speak to your teacher
If you've checked everything above and the problem is still occurring:
- Tell them the transaction or attendance entry you are trying to report.
- Tell them the issue category you selected and the message you wrote.
