# Accounts and Transfers

## Overview
The Accounts tab allows you to view your current checking and savings balances, transfer funds between them, and review your complete transaction history.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Viewing Balances and History
1. Navigate to **Accounts** from the student sidebar.
2. The main view displays your **Checking** balance (money ready to spend), **Savings** balance (money set aside to earn interest), and your overall **Total Balance**.
3. Scroll down or click the **Transactions** tab to review all your recent income and expenses.

### Transferring Funds
1. While on the **Accounts** page, click the **Transfer** tab.
2. Select the direction you want to move money (Checking to Savings, or Savings to Checking).
3. Enter the amount you wish to transfer.
4. Submit the transfer. Your balances will update immediately.

## Important Notes
> [!NOTE]
> **Plan ahead:** Remember that money in your Savings account earns interest, but you usually cannot spend it directly in the Store or use it to pay Rent. You must transfer it back to Checking first.
