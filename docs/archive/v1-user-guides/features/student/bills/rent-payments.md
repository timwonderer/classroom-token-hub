# Pay Rent

## Overview
Your rent charges and due dates appear in the Bills section. Paying rent on time ensures you maintain access to classroom privileges and avoids late penalties.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Making a Rent Payment
1. Navigate to **Bills** from the student sidebar.
2. Under the Rent section, review your pending rent charges. Click on a charge to see the **What Rent Covers** list (this shows exactly what value you are getting for your money, like desk access or free passes).
3. If you have enough funds in your Checking account, click **Pay Rent** to complete the transaction.

## Important Notes
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Understanding Coverage Periods:** Rent payments cover a specific date range. You are only considered "Rent Active" if today's date falls within a paid coverage period. You cannot pre-pay for future months while skipping your current overdue rent.

> [!TIP]
> **If you cannot pay:** If you fall behind on rent and lose your primary privileges, check the Store for "Rent Alternatives." You can often buy back essential privileges individually while you save up enough money to pay your full rent bill.
