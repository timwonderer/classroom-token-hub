# Customizing Rent Behaviors

## Overview
Rent behavior settings allow you to finely control the strictness of your rent collection, including grace periods, late penalties, and purchase restrictions.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Adjusting Rules and Penalties
1. Navigate to **Bills > Rent** in the teacher sidebar and open the **Settings** tab.
2. Adjust the **Grace Period** to define how many days students have to pay rent after it is issued before penalties begin.
3. Configure the **Late Penalty** amount and how frequently it recurs (e.g., a one-time fee vs. a daily escalating fine).
4. Toggle **Store Purchase Restrictions** if you want to block students from buying fun items while their rent is actively overdue.
5. Save your changes to apply the new behaviors to the next billing cycle.

## Important Notes
> [!TIP]
> **Classroom Norms:** Align these penalties with your actual classroom management style. If you are generally lenient with deadlines, configure a longer grace period so the economy feels fair rather than punishing.
