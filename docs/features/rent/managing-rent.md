# Managing Rent Settings

Configure how rent is collected, set amounts, and manage schedules for your classroom economy.

## Quick Start

1. Go to **Rent** -> **Settings** tab.
2. Toggle **Enable Rent System**.
3. Select a **Class/Block** to configure.
4. Set **Rent Amount** and **Frequency**.
5. Save changes.

## Configuration Options

### 1. Per-Block Settings
Rent settings can be customized for each class block. Use the **Class Selector** (e.g., "Period 1", "Period 2") at the top of the settings form to switch between blocks. You can:
- Enable rent for one block but keep it disabled for another.
- Set different rent amounts for different blocks (e.g., AP classes pay more).
- Use different due dates or frequencies.

### 2. Rent Amount & Schedule
- **Rent Amount**: The base amount students owe per period.
- **Frequency**: How often rent is charged (Weekly, Bi-weekly, Monthly).
- **First Due Date**: The date of the first rent charge.
- **Grace Period**: Number of days after the due date before late fees apply.

### 3. Penalties & Restrictions
- **Late Penalty**: One-time or recurring fee for late payments.
- **Block Store Purchases**: Prevent students from buying items in the store if they have overdue rent. This is a powerful incentive for timely payments.

## Best Practices
- **Start Simple**: Begin with a low monthly rent and no penalties to let students adjust.
- **Differentiate**: If appropriate, vary rent based on class "difficulty" or "salary" levels using the per-block settings.
- **Communicate**: Let students know when rent is due and what the late policy is.

## Troubleshooting
- **Rent not charging?** Check the "Next Due Date" in settings to ensure it's in the future.
- **Students can't pay?** Ensure they have enough funds in their Checking account, or use the "Waivers" tab to excuse a payment.
