# Customizing Rent Behaviors

Use rent behavior settings to control how strict rent collection is.

## Where to work
Go to **Bills > Rent** and open the **Settings** tab.

## Common behavior controls
- Grace period before late penalties apply.
- Late penalty amount and recurrence.
- Store purchase restrictions for overdue rent.
- Waivers for excused or special cases.

## Tips
Align penalties with your classroom norms so rent feels fair and predictable.
