# Feature Toggles

## Overview
Feature toggles allow you to customize the platform to fit your specific classroom economy plan. You can enable or disable optional modules to reduce clutter for features you aren't using.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Toggling Features
1. Navigate to **Settings > Economy Features** in the teacher sidebar.
2. Review the list of available modules (e.g., Banking, Rent, Store).
3. Toggle features on or off depending on what you plan to use with your class.
4. Changes are applied immediately and will show or hide the corresponding tabs for both you and your students.

## Important Notes
> [!TIP]
> **Start Simple:** If you are new to classroom economies, start with just Payroll and Store unlocked. Turn on Rent and Banking once your students are comfortable with the basics.
