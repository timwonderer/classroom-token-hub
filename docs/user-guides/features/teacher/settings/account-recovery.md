# Account Recovery

## Overview
Teacher account recovery utilizes a secure student-assisted verification method. This ensures that you can regain access to your account without the platform needing to store sensitive personal contact information like emails or phone numbers.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Setting Up Account Recovery
1. Open the teacher **Dashboard** and look for the account recovery setup prompt, or go directly to the recovery setup page when prompted by the app.
2. Use the date picker to enter your date of birth and select **Save & Enable Recovery**.
3. Recovery then relies on your saved date of birth plus student-assisted verification if you ever need to regain access.

## Important Notes
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Keep recovery enabled and complete setup when prompted on the dashboard, especially on older teacher accounts that were created before this flow was added.
