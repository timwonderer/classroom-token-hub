# Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
The Analytics Dashboard summarizes system health and economy behavior across your classes. Use these metrics to quickly identify trends in balances, rent compliance, and payroll efficiency.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Viewing Analytics
1. Navigate to **Economy > Analytics** in the teacher sidebar.
2. Review the high-level system health signals and alerts.
3. Scroll down to examine specific economy trends regarding balances, rent collection, and payroll distribution.
4. Toggle between class periods to view class-level summaries or dive into individual student metrics.

## Important Notes
> [!TIP]
> **Check frequently:** Regular checks of the analytics dashboard can help you spot economic imbalances (like inflation) before they impact student motivation.
