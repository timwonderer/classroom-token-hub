# Pay Rent

Your rent charges and due dates appear in the Bills section.

## Where to work
Go to **Bills** from the student sidebar.

## Pay rent
Select a rent charge and confirm payment when you have enough balance.

## If you cannot pay
Ask your teacher about waivers or due date adjustments.
