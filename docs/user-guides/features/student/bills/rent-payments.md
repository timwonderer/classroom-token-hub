# Pay Rent

Your rent charges and due dates appear in the Bills section. Paying rent enables access to classroom privileges and store items.

## Where to work
Go to **Bills** from the student sidebar.

## Understanding Rent Coverage

Rent payments now cover a specific date range, known as your **Coverage Period**.

- **Start Date:** The rent due date the payment is applied to.
- **End Date:** The day before the next rent due date.

**Important:** You are only considered "Rent Active" if today's date falls within a paid coverage period. If you are late, your privileges (like desk access) may expire until you pay.

## What Rent Covers

Your teacher may break down rent into specific items. These can grant you different benefits:

### 1. Privilege Items (e.g., Desk, Locker)
- **Included:** If you pay rent, you get these automatically.
- **Expiration:** Valid until your rent coverage ends.
- **Store Alternative:** If you don't pay rent, you may be able to buy these individually in the store, but they usually cost more than the rent bundle.

### 2. Per-Use Items (e.g., Supplies, One-Time Passes)
- **Included:** Rent payers may get a set number of "Free Uses" (e.g., 5 free pencils).
- **Store Alternative:** Once you use up your free allowance (or if you don't pay rent), you must buy these in the store.

### 3. Hall Passes
- **Included:** Paying rent may add extra hall passes to your balance.

## Pay rent
1. Review the **What Rent Covers** list to see the value you are getting.
2. Select a rent charge.
3. Confirm payment. You need enough balance in your Checking account.

## If you cannot pay
Ask your teacher about waivers or due date adjustments. If you are late, remember you can still purchase **Rent Alternative** items in the store to keep your essential privileges (like your desk) while you save up for the full rent.
