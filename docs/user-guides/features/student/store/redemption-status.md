# Track Redemptions

## Overview
Some store purchases (like physical items or special privileges) require your teacher to manually approve and hand them out to you. You can track the status of these purchases directly.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Checking Your Purchase Status
1. Navigate to the **Store** from the student sidebar.
2. Look for the section marking your recent purchases and redemptions.
3. Review the status indicators:
   - **Pending:** Your teacher has received the order but has not approved/delivered it yet.
   - **Completed:** Your teacher has marked the item as delivered or active.

## Important Notes
> [!TIP]
> **Stuck redemptions:** If an item remains "Pending" for a long time and you have not received it physically in class, you can submit an Issue Report to politely remind your teacher.
