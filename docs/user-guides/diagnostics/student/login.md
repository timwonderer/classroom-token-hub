# Troubleshooting Login and Setup

If something doesn't look right with your Login and Setup, check this guide first before reporting an issue to your teacher.

## If you cannot log in, check these first:
- Are you using the exact username assigned during setup? (No nicknames).
- Are you entering your PIN, not your passphrase?
- Did you complete setup in this order? Claim account -> create username -> set PIN/passphrase.
- Did the CAPTCHA (Turnstile) load and verify before login?
- Are you trying to use a join code to log in? Join codes are only for claiming or adding classes, not logging in.
- Are you trying to use your passphrase at login? Passphrases are only for purchases and transfers.

## This is normal and expected when:
- You are sent to Complete Profile if your account is missing birth date details.
- Login is temporarily blocked after too many attempts (rate limit).
- You see a Claim Account button if your teacher created your account but you have not claimed it yet.

## When to report an issue to your teacher
If you've checked everything above and the problem is still occurring:
- Tell them the exact username you typed and the error text.
- Tell them whether the CAPTCHA widget appeared and was checked.
- Tell them whether you completed the setup flow before logging in.
- Use the "Report Issue" button on the Help page.
