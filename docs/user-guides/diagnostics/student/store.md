# Troubleshooting Store and Purchases

If something doesn't look right with your Store and Purchases, check this guide first before reporting an issue to your teacher.

## If an item is missing from the store, check these first:
- Is the store enabled for your class period?
- Is the item active and not past its auto-delist date?
- Is the item visible to your class block? Some items are block-specific.
- Are you trying to purchase an item from another class? Items are scoped to your current class.

## If a purchase fails, check these first:
- Did you enter the correct passphrase?
- Do you have enough funds, or do overdraft settings allow it?
- Are you late on rent, and are purchases restricted when late?
- Have you hit the per-student purchase limit?
- Is the inventory empty? Inventory may be zero even if the item still shows.

## This is normal and expected when:
- Immediate items redeem right away, while delayed items require teacher approval.
- Collective items sit in a pending state until the goal is reached.
- Hall pass items add passes to your balance instead of doing a standard redemption.

## When to report an issue to your teacher
If you've checked everything above and the problem is still occurring:
- Tell them the item name, price, and error message.
- Tell them whether your class rent status shows late.
- Use the "Report Issue" button on the Help page.
