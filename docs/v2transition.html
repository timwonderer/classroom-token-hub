Classroom Token Hub

Version 1 End of Service

On June 30,2026, Version 1 of Classroom Token Hub reached the end of service with its final release of v1.9.1.

During the short run of Version 1 (6 months), more than 200 students participated on the platform and surfaced insights, assumptions, and hidden violations that would not have been found without them. Many features and stabilizing mechanisms were developed based on their feedback and informal "pentests".

After 1000+ commits and 500+ deployments, Classroom Token Hub has become a full-fledge classroom economy system that is unique, and perhaps first of its kind, in the market. But our core principle remains the same: to provide a free and open platform for teachers to run their classroom economies. A platform that respects privacy, invariant, and observability so teachers and students can trust the system.

Thank you for your support. This platform became better because of its users.

Classroom Token Hub isn't going away.
