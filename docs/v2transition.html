Version 1 End of Service Notice

Currently Building v2.0

On July 1, 2026 at 12:00 AM PDT, Version 1 of Classroom Token Hub reached the end of service.

Just because Version 1 operation has ended, it doesn't mean Classroom Token Hub is going away too.

Classroom Token Hub started off as a personal project that slowly grew in scope. 9 months ago, Classroom Token Hub was a monolith meant for a single teacher with seven classes. 6 months ago, Classroom Token Hub was officially launched with real student users. 3 months ago, Classroom Token Hub stabilized with less than 5 errors over a 30-day period. Now, we are ready to grow out of our legacy architecture and formally evolve.

During the short run of Version 1 (6 months), more than 200 students participated on the platform and surfaced insights, assumptions, and hidden violations that would not have been found without them. Many features and stabilizing mechanisms were developed based on their feedback and informal "pentests".

After 1000+ commits and 500+ deployments, Classroom Token Hub has become a full-fledged classroom economy system that is unique, and perhaps first of its kind, in the market. But our core principle remains the same: to provide a free and open platform for teachers to run their classroom economies. A platform that respects privacy, invariants, and observability so teachers and students can trust the system.

Thank you for your support. This platform became better because of its users. We can't wait to share the next evolution of Classroom Token Hub with you. Stay tuned.

v1 Lesson: The Trap of "One-More-Feature"

Sometimes the right question isn't "how do we build it" but "should we build it?"

During the development of Version 1, we were focused on pushing as many features as possible because we equate freedom with funtionality. In the process, we inadvertently shipped features that violated our own invariants. Many of those features were never used after six months.The truth is, teachers and students are not looking for apps that offer the most features; on the contrary, they want simple, reliable systems that meet their core needs.

v2 Changes: Stronger Foundation, Faster Resolutions

If you use this app without second thoughts, we've done it.

At launch, v2.0 will not have new features; instead, it's the power that's inside. We rebuild the architecture to make core business logic more robust and maintainable. That means teachers can rest easy knowing their classrom economy is reliable and students can trust their accounts to be accurate. When downtime does occur, expect much shorter resolution times thanks to better error reporting on the backend.
