First time here? Come on in.

Classroom Token Hub is classroom reward management system specifically designed to simulate real world economics. Teachers can implement bills, payrolls, store, hall pass management, and individual simulated bank accounts. Students learn by participating in paying rent, buying insurance, tracking time, making purchases, and more.

Our goal is to provide a free, reliable, secure, and easy to use classroom economy system for students grade 5 and up. Many of our features were designed around observed student behaviors and potential teacher fatigue. Instead of features, we focus on guardrails. We traded unlimited customizability for bounded personalization.

The result is a deterministic, straight-forward, and privacy-first classroom economy platform with just enough customization to fit unique classroom needs.
