How to: Store Management

Manage store items, approve redemptions, and review purchase history.

Start here to get your store running. Use the Manage Items tab to add and activate items.

  • Name/description: What students see in the store.
  • Price & tier: Sets cost and grouping for your pricing structure.
  • Item type: Immediate vs delayed redemption.
  • Active: Inactive items are hidden from students.
  • Class visibility: Limit items to specific periods.
  • Inventory: Caps total redemptions available.
  • Limit per student: Restricts how many times each student can redeem.
Tip: Keep prices aligned to your CWI so rewards feel achievable.

  • Bundles: Set quantity per purchase for multi-use items.
  • Bulk discount: Lower price when students buy in larger quantities.
  • Collective goal: Require class-wide progress before redemption.
  • Auto delist/expiry: Remove items after a date or number of days.
  • Redemption prompt: Collect details from students at checkout.
Side effects: Deactivating or delisting items hides them immediately; inventory limits can block redemptions once exhausted.

Review redemption details and approve or deny requests from the overview panel.

Use the Purchase History tab to see what students have redeemed and when.
