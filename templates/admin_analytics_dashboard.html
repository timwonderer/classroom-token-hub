How to: Analytics

The Analytics Dashboard is your "check engine light" for the economy. It helps you see if things are running smoothly without grading or ranking students.

These metrics represent the heartbeat of your economy:

  • Participation Rate: Are students logging in and doing things? Active is good!
  • Money Velocity: Are transactions happening? Low velocity means money is stagnant (hoarding or nothing to buy).

All values are relative to your Class Wealth Index (CWI):

  • On-Track Students: Shows how many students are earning close to what you designed.
  • Budget Survival: Can students pay rent/insurance and still save 10%? If this is low, rent might be too high.

  • Green Healthy: Everything is working as designed.
  • Yellow Warning: Something is drifting (e.g., velocity slowing down). Check it out soon.
  • Red Critical: Immediate action needed (e.g., most students can't afford rent).

Knowing what "lever to pull" is key:

  • Too many poor students? Lower rent, raise wages, or add bonus jobs.
  • Too much hoarding? Add fun store items or limited-time sales.
  • Low participation? Remind students about payroll or upcoming store openings.
