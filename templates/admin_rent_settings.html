How to: Rent Management

Configure rent rules, review collection status, and manage waivers.

Use the Settings tab to turn rent on, set frequency, and define the rent amount.

Reminder: When rent is disabled, students will not see rent or rent notifications.

  • Class/Block selector: switch between blocks to configure rent independently (e.g., enable for Period 1, disable for Period 2).
  • Rent amount: the base charge students owe each rent period.
  • Frequency: daily, weekly, monthly, or custom intervals; controls how often rent posts.
  • First due date / due day: sets the first billing date or the day of month rent is due.
  • Grace period: days after the due date before late penalties apply.
  • Late penalty: amount charged when rent is late; can apply once or recur.
  • Recurring penalty frequency: how often the late fee repeats (when recurring is enabled).
  • Bill preview: shows upcoming rent to students a set number of days early.
  • Incremental payments: allows partial payments instead of a single full payment.
  • Block purchases when late: prevents store purchases if rent is overdue.
Side effects: Changing frequency or due dates can shift when students are billed next.

Check paid vs. unpaid totals to understand how rent is impacting the class.

Add waivers for special cases or missed periods in the Waivers tab.

Use Economy Health to check whether rent aligns to student earnings.
