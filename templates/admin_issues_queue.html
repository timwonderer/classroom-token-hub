How to: Student Issues

Review student issue tickets, resolve them quickly, or escalate to developers when needed.

Open any ticket in the Pending Review tab to read the student explanation and view related details.

Choose the appropriate resolution option and leave a clear note for the student.

Tip: Clear notes help students understand what changed and what to do next.

Escalate technical issues and include any steps you tried or patterns you noticed.

Reminder: Students cannot contact developers directly, so your detail matters.

Students see a status badge for each ticket:

  • Open Ticket is awaiting teacher review.
  • Teacher Review Teacher is working on the issue.
  • Escalated To Dev Escalated to developers.
  • Dev Resolved Developer fix applied; teacher final review required.
  • Teacher Final Review Teacher confirms classroom state and may close.
  • Closed Ticket has been closed by teacher.

Escalated issues include a non-identifying student reference and your notes.

Privacy: Developers never see student names or other identifying details.
