How to: Insurance Management

Create insurance policies, enroll students, and review claims.

Create or update policies in the Existing Policies or Add New Policy tabs.

  • Premium: amount charged per cycle to stay enrolled.
  • Frequency: how often premiums are collected.
  • Claim type: determines whether payouts are monetary or non-monetary.
  • Waiting period: days before a student can submit a claim.
  • Max claims: caps total claims per period or overall.
  • Status: active policies can be enrolled; inactive policies are hidden.
Side effects: Changing policy terms affects future enrollments and claims.

Use the Active Student Policies tab to see who is enrolled and manage coverage.

Open the Claims tab to review and resolve pending requests.

Tip: Claims follow the waiting period set on each policy.

Compare premium costs with CWI to keep coverage affordable.
