Troubleshooting Steps
  • • Check your account type - you might be logged in as a student but trying to access an admin-only page (or vice versa)
  • • Re-authenticate - your session may have expired. Try logging out and logging back in
  • • Verify the URL - make sure you're accessing the correct page for your account type
  • • Contact your teacher/admin - if you believe you should have access, ask them to check your permissions
  • • Clear your browser cache - sometimes old session data can cause permission issues
